How to use

1
Make sure your phone and this PC are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
2
Click "Generate QR Code" on this screen.
3
Open your phone camera and scan the QR code. A web page will open automatically.
4
Tap "Select Photos", choose the photos you want to send, then tap "Send".
5
Photos are saved automatically to C:\MobileUpload on this PC.
⚠ Note: The QR code expires after 5 minutes. If it expires, click Reset and generate a new one.

Each transfer session accepts connections only from your local network (Wi-Fi). Your files never leave your network.
